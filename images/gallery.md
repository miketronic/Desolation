## Desolation V3 - Updates!
- Hot swap sockets!
- More polished
- Longer solder nuts
- Option for power toggle (top or bottom mount) or power button.
- Option for soft or tactile reset button, and top or bottom mount.
- Able to flush undermount the MCU.
